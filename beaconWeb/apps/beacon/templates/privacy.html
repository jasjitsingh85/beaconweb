Hotspot Privacy Policy

COLLECTED INFORMATION PROVIDED BY USER

Hotspot requires several pieces of personal information in order to operate as designed. Name and active phone number are required. We may, however, request other information such as your email address.

ADDITIONAL COLLECTED INFORMATION

Hotspot will also collect non-personally identifiable information as part of the service. This will typically include browser type, language preference, IP address, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Hotspot collects this data to optimize our service to users.

Hotspot may also use cookies or web Hotspot to track a user's interactions with the Service. This information will be used for internal purposes only to optimize the Hotspot Service. Users can opt-out of cookies by utilizing the settings provided in their browsers.

Additionally, Hotspot collects information from its mobile phone users (who have installed the Hotspot application) in a similar manner to optimize the mobile experience. Beyond mobile phone numbers, we collect mobile screen resolution, device make and manufacturer, as well as the device's unique identifier (in the iPhone's case, a UDID).

USE AND DISCLOSURE OF COLLECTED INFORMATION

Hotspot uses provided and collected information only for internal purposes and does not disclose data to third parties. Hotspot may occasionally inform users of new releases and features via any communication methods you have provided to the Service.

YOUR RESPONSIBILITY ONLINE

You should remain aware that any information you share online or via text message can be accessed by others. Hotspot is not responsible for the actions of those who obtain your content in this manner. Hotspot cannot guarantee your safety and security and you should be aware that submitting any information and using the Hotspot Service is done at your own risk.

CHANGES TO THE PRIVACY POLICY

Although most changes are likely to be minor, Hotspot's may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in Hotspot's sole discretion. Hotspot encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
